Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book?
We recommend booking at least 6–12 months in advance to secure your date. Popular dates in Niagara — particularly summer and fall — fill quickly.
Do you travel outside Niagara?
Absolutely. We regularly work across Hamilton, the GTA, Toronto, and beyond. For weddings over 2–3 hours away we may require overnight accommodation — we'll discuss this during your consultation.
Can we hire Captivate for just photo or just video?
Yes. We play well with others. We may not be the right fit for every vision, so we want you to have the perfect people for every job. Photo-only and video-only packages are available.
What is the +Anticipate engagement shoot?
It's a pre-wedding session designed to get you comfortable in front of the camera. Couples who do it consistently say their wedding day felt more natural and relaxed. We highly recommend it.

 

Do you offer same-day edits?
Yes — our same-day edit packages allow a short highlight film to be played at your reception. Guests love it, and it's something you'll rewatch forever.
Who owns the rights to our photos and videos?
Captivate retains the rights to use imagery for advertising and portfolio purposes. You receive a USB with your edited images and videos, with no watermarks.
Are packages customizable?
Absolutely. Every package can be adjusted — more or fewer hours, adding or removing the engagement shoot, raw footage options. Book a free consultation and we'll build something that works for you.
Do you offer drone videography at weddings?
Yes. Our licensed drone operators provide breathtaking aerial footage of your venue and surroundings. Ask about adding drone coverage to your package.
